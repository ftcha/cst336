Firewalls

As the internet continues to grow and technologies evolves,
threats are evolving as quickly. The traditional firewall with
typical functions such as packet filtering, NAT, VPN, and stateful
inspection can only do so much against all the new threats. This
forces firewall manufactures to rethink and retool firewalls
thus resulting in next generation firewalls.

Next-generation Firewalls (NGFW) are the latest firewalls
available to consumers and busineses. NGFWs combines multiple
technologies such as intrusion prevention, intrusion detection,
and deep packet inspections. These technologies works together
to detect threats quicker, providing greater protection
