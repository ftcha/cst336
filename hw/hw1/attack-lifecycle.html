Attack Lifecycle

The following steps are consider the attack lifecyle of any cyber attacks. Cyber attackers strategize using this lifecycle.

Reconnaissance: Attackers carefully plan their attacks, by reserching their identified targets.
Weaponization and Delivery: Attackers determine which attack method to use.
Exploitation: Attackers deploy exploits again applications and systems.
Installation: Attackers will install malware in order conduct further operations.
Command and Control: Attackers can now control the systems.
Actions on the Objective: Attackers will now act upon their motivations.
